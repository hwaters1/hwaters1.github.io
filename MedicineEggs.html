What make eggs so great for your health

The answer is quite simple. Vitamins. Vitamins are an essential nutrient in your body as
they perform many vital roles, fighting disease, repairing your muscles, strengthening your
joints. Eggs can contain so many vital vitamins if they are prepared the right way.

What vitamins can I expect.

Eggs are extremely rich in many vitamins. The most notable being Vitamin D, eggs contain
anywhere from 37IU - 67IU of Vitamin D. IU is a pharmaceutical unit of measurement used
to measure prescriptions and vitamins. Vitamin D is important in the body because it
regulates calcium and phosphate levels, without it, bone deformities can occur, teeth
bones, and muscles all rely on Vitamin D, and that is juat one essential nutrient in eggs.
Eggs also contain: Vitamin A, Vitamin K Vitamin B5, Vitamin B12, Vitamin E, Vitamin B6,
Calcium, Phosphorus, and Selenium.
