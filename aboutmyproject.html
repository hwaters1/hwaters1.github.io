Problem Solving

I came across many problems throughout this project, anywhere from as simple
as putting a break in a paragraph to somewhat more difficult CSS problems such
as changing colour when hovered over. Usually I solved problems just by trying
to isolate the issue and find out what part was specifically wrong. Although
some problems weren't logic problems and were instead problems with the form.
Many times the closing tags didn't include a '/' and therefore didn't work, I
didn't realize this and therefore ended up very confused. There were a lot of
online resources that I used, the most notable being Stack Overflow, W3Schools
and freeCodeCamp. Stack Overflow is a basic website where any user can ask
any question, at any level, in any coding language, Although I didn't ask
any questions I could search through the archives of the previously asked
questions where people had similar questions and were provided with answers.

An understanding of web design. Why did I like my website?

Throughout this project I believe my knowledge and understanding of web design has grown greatly.
Before this project I knew some small amounts of HTML although my primary focus has always been C#
I learnt a lot about CSS which I think will be very useful later on as I plan to software developer
and having the abilities to develop a great website is the first step for that. What do I like about my
website? First off I think the text that reacts when you hover over it, by changing colour, is a really
cool aesthetic feature and it springs life into the website. I think the colour scheme of soft pastel
colours work really well together and they form a very calm and clean learning environment. I think
people would come to this website if they wish to gain the basic understanding of eggs and their
relevancy. The fonts work very well together in the website, and the addition of a text shado
really gives the page more atmosphere, Overall I believe I should receive a level 4 on this project
I put lots of time into the aesthetic design of the website and flow of the website. The fonts all
work very well together and I believe this adds a deeper aspect of design to the website. Other than
that I believe I have met every other requirement on the page
