What are puns?

Puns are a basic form of humour. Originally denounced by Dr. Samuel James as the
lowest form of comedy, they have since risen and are now regarded as an almost not-awful
form of comedy.

Why egg puns?

Eggs are just wonderful for puns. The word 'egg' can be substituted for the letters 'ex' as in
execute, exceed, excite and many more words with the letters ex. This means there can be a great
number of puns about eggs. But that isn't it. The gelatine-like center of an egg, the 'yolk' is
also a way to form puns and jokes as the word 'yolk' sounds a lot like 'joke' when verbalized
This can create many 'meta-jokes' which are jokes that break the fourth wall, usually a 'meta-joke'
is a joke, in which the punchline is based around the joke being a joke, which makes an excellent joke.
